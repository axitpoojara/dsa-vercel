About DSA

Darshil Shah & Associates is India based emerging Chartered Accountant firm giving best services in India and across the globe. The journey has began in 1955 and today the firm has achieved various milestones in professional services like Accounting-Bookkeeping-Outsourcing, Audit & Assurance, Legal Advisory, Direct & Indirect Taxation, Valuations and Finance consultancy.


The objective of the firm is to fulfill clients’ rudiments at best possible manner with flexible and proactive approach which goes with the changing environment. We envisage keeping the interest of our clients at bulls eye before the firm’s interest.


Our core team of professionals have individual experience and expertise in various fields which gives the clients an added advantage. The guiding philosophy of the firm is to render specialized and tailormade services.
